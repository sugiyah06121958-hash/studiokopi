# **App Name**: KopiLoka Landing

## Core Features:

- Product Showcase: Visually present ground and roasted coffee with descriptions, origins, tasting notes, and a WhatsApp link for each product.
- Storytelling Section: Showcase the brand's story and the coffee-making process from bean to cup.
- AI Recipe Suggestion: AI tool recommends coffee recipes based on the user's preferences.
- Testimonial Carousel: Display customer testimonials and reviews to build trust and credibility.
- FAQ Section: Answer common questions about coffee origins, roasting processes, and product details.
- Contact Form: A simple form to capture user inquiries.
- Social Media Integration: Link to social media platforms to expand brand visibility and engagement.
- Secure Payment Gateway: Integrate a secure payment gateway to process online transactions safely and efficiently.
- Order Tracking: Enable customers to track their orders in real-time.
- Promotional Banners: Highlight special offers, discounts, and new arrivals with visually appealing promotional banners.
- Customer Account Management: Allow customers to create and manage their accounts, save addresses, and view order history.
- Email Marketing Integration: Integrate with email marketing platforms to send newsletters, promotional emails, and order updates.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Warm brown (#A67B5B) to reflect the natural coffee beans.
- Background color: Light beige (#F5F5DC) for a cozy and inviting atmosphere.
- Accent color: Deep red-brown (#8B4513) for highlighting key sections and calls to action.
- Body text: 'Literata', a serif font, will create a literary and vintage atmosphere.
- Headline font: 'Belleza', a sans-serif font with a style aligned to art and design will be paired with Literata.
- Use hand-drawn icons related to coffee, such as coffee beans, cups, and roasting equipment.
- Employ a clean, grid-based layout with plenty of white space to provide easy readability and visual hierarchy.
- Use subtle animations on image hover to engage visitors. Consider parallax scrolling for an immersive experience.
